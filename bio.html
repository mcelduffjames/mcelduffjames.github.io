James McElduff

Biography

James grew up in Mountain Lakes, NJ with two older brothers, Matthew and Michael. In his early years, James enjoyed playing with Legos and Playmobil with his friends and brothers. He also was involved with youth athletics, mainly swimming and baseball. James reached the peak of his popularity in 5th grade when he was class and School President of Wildwood Elementary School.

James continued his academic and athletic career at Briarcliff Middle School, where he found an interest in Computer class. James also found an increased interest in Club Swimming, which lead to a decline in interest for baseball. James was excited to join his two older brothers at Mountain Lakes High School in 2010.

James attended Mountain Lakes High School from 2010 - 2014. He continued to swim for the club swimming team and joined the High School Swim team. James took challenging classes throughout highschool and decided to pursue a future of rigorous academics and athletics at Tufts University. James is currently attending Tufts University and plans to graduate in 2018 with a major in Quantitative Economics and a minor in Computer Science.
